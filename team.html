The team

Amanda Azzali

Child Protection · Migration · Francophone WCA · Middle East

Amanda holds a degree in International Relations and Diplomatic Sciences from the University of Trieste and an Advanced Specialization in International Cooperation from ENFAP Emilia Romagna. Over twenty years, she has dedicated her work to the protection of the most marginalised and vulnerable children across three continents. She would do it all over again.

Her particular combination — CP technical standard-setting, migration programming, nexus programme architecture, and regional capacity building — spans West and Central Africa and the Middle East. She is fluent in English, French, and Italian.
